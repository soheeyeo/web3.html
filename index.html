Feel the Beauty of Paris

Paris, with its inspiring grandeur and mesmerizing charm never fails to have an indelible imprint on the minds and in the hearts of every individual that makes a visit to this alluring France capital. Be it travel, food or fashion, the city never ceases to disappoint. The land of love and romance, it’s amorous essence can be felt through the ever inspiring and exhilarating works of Hemingway and Baldaccini. Let’s dig into this captivating world of enchantment and fall in love with Paris!

Christmas and New Year in Paris

As is the custom each year, the city is decked out in lights as the festive season approaches. Streets sparkle, major department stores come alive and the magic of Christmas weaves its spell! Christmas markets, nativity scenes, concerts and a multitude of celebrations offer a wide choice of activities for the whole family. This festive atmosphere is also experienced in the restaurants where exceptional menus are offered for an unforgettable Christmas Eve. Everyone can enjoy the festive holiday atmosphere in Paris.
